## Adding projects
Edit `src/data/proyectos.json`. Each entry needs `title`, `image`, `description`, `category`, `tags[]`, and `links` (with optional `website` and `github`). Category badges use inline ternaries in the template (note: there's a `categoryColors` map in frontmatter that is unused вҖ” the template duplicates its logic inline).
